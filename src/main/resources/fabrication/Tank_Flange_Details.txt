Tank Flange (Curb) Details: 

tank_Flg_W:

For 400kVA and below, Curb Width = 40 mm 
For 401 kVA to 630 kVA, Curb Width = 50 mm 
For 631 kVA to 2000 kVA, Curb Width = 65 mm 
For 2001 kVA to 6300 kVA, Curb Width = 75 mm 
For 6301 kVA to 8000 kVA, Curb Width = 80 mm
For 8001 kVA and above, Curb Width = 100 mm 


tank_Flg_Bolt_Dia:

Up to 5000kVA, Hole diameter = 12 mm 
From 5001 kVA to 7000 kVA, Hole diameter = 14 mm 
Above 7000 kVA, Hole diameter = 18 mm
